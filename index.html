ABOUT OUR HOTEL

Our Hotel is located in the heart of Mombasa. It is the most modern hotel in the region. Since the year 2000 we have been a pioneer in the hospitality industry within the region.
We boast of 89 luxury rooms, 4 junior suites and a luxurious executive suite. The spacious rooms are tastefully furnished with different colors, giving you the feel of being in a home away from home. The interior is a blend of comfort and convenience, with fine food, a battalion of friendly staff and a unique touch that turn a simple break into a serious treat.
